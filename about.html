Our Visionary Journey

Expertise
& Innovation

Green UTOPIA was born from the convergence of scientific rigor and professional haircare field expertise. Located in the heart of Portugal, our laboratory relies on a unique alliance between two complementary profiles sharing the same exacting standards of quality. One, a former hairdresser specializing in salon work, masters the real expectations of women, the sensoriality of textures, and the importance of immediate results. The other, a biologist expert in new cosmetic technologies, guarantees the performance, safety, and stability of formulas. Together, we have transformed our production unit into an incubator of excellence, dedicated to creating hair solutions conceived by professionals, for professionals, and validated by science.
